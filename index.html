Visualizing Housing Market Trends: An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features

Visualizing Housing Market Trends: An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features using Tableau

This project focuses on analyzing housing market trends by examining property features and sale prices using Tableau. The dataset was cleaned and prepared to ensure accuracy, including verification of data types, removal of null values, and validation of total records loaded. Key metrics such as total properties, average sale price, and basement area were calculated to gain meaningful insights.

  • Analyzed housing market data to understand pricing trends and property features.
  • Cleaned and prepared the dataset for accurate visualization in Tableau.
  • Created interactive dashboards to explore sales performance and housing characteristics.

Interactive dashboards were created to visualize relationships between price, location, property size, and other housing characteristics. The analysis helps identify pricing patterns and performance trends within the housing market, making the data easy to understand and interpret through clear and structured visualizations.
